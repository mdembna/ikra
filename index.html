About me

Izabela Krywiczanin is a young artist from Warsaw. Her paintings are strongly influenced by Magdalena Dembna, who is her idol and the only god she believes in. Izabela's passion for coffee and the Charlotte's breakfast is legendary. I wouldn't be suprised if she used white chocolate and raspberries instead of white and pink paint. She is currently involved with another artist - Patryk Wasilewski, known as "Patryś". She has an astonishing collection of pictures of sleeping Patryś, which will be displayed on this website unless she finds time to have coffee with me next week!
